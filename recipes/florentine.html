Eggs Florentine

Description

Invented in the streets of Florence which many of you must have visited while traveling and others, in a more elegant way, must have visited while playing Assassin's Creed II, this recipe packs the sweetness and a bit of saltiness of Florence, Italy.

This mouth-watering dish will produce 3 servings and will take only 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 (10 ounce) packaged fresh spinach
  • 6 large eggs, slightly beaten
  • Salt to taste
  • Ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons cream cheese

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Cook and stir mushrooms and garlic until garlic produces aroma ~about 1 minute.
  3. Add spinach to mushroom mixture and cook until spinach is wilted ~2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Stir eggs into mushroom-spinach mixture, season it with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook without stirring until eggs become firm and then flip.
  6. Sprinkle cream cheese over egg mixture.
  7. Cook until cream cheese starts to soften ~about 5 minutes.
