Cream Cheese and Tomato Omelet with Chives

Description

A light, fluffy and delicious omelet! Perfectly served with tomatoes and chives. Use garlic chives to make this omelet extra tasty. This dish will have 2 servings (you can tinker with the ingredients if you want more) and will take 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs

  • 1 tablespoon milk

  • Salt to taste

  • Ground black pepper to taste

  • 3 tablespoons softened cream cheese

  • 2 seeded and diced tomatoes

  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.

  2. Heat a 6-inch non-stick skillet over medium heat.

  3. Pour egg mixture into the hot skillet, then keep tilting the skillet so egg mixture covers the entire floor of the skillet.

  4. Slowly cook egg mixture until set ~5 to 10 minutes.

  5. Arrange small dollops of cream cheese onto half the omelet.

  6. Sprinkle tomato and chives over cream cheese.

  7. Fold omelet in half over the fillings.

  8. Remove skillet from the stove and cover until cream cheese has softened ~2 to 3 minutes.
